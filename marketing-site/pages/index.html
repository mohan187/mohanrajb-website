About Me.

I am a DevOps, CloudOps, and Platform Engineer with 4+ years of experience building secure and maintainable cloud platforms on GCP and AWS. My day-to-day work covers Terraform-based infrastructure, CI/CD automation, Kubernetes operations, IAM, observability, Linux-based platform support, and production readiness for multi-environment systems.

Most recently, I worked on the Costco Wholesale AI/ML program, where I helped build enterprise-grade GCP environments across ADT, SPT, and PRD with PAM-based access controls, GitHub Actions delivery using Workload Identity Federation, and deployment workflows for BigQuery, Dataproc, Composer, Vertex AI, GCS, and Secret Manager.

I have also spent a strong part of my recent work on data-related platforms, including Azure ADLS to GCP migration through Composer DAGs, BigQuery-based analytics support, feature-engineering pipelines, Cloud SQL and Redis-backed runtime tuning, and cross-project IAM for data and ML teams.
